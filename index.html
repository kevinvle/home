How we started

  • August 6th 2016, 3:00 AM PST

    Our Humble Beginning

    Give Hope started in Kevin's bedroom one late night when he was contemplating how he could help out the homeless people in Milpitas, CA and Santa Cruz, CA. He knew he can make an impact in others' lives using technology widely available to him.

  • August 8th, 2016

    Phase One Expansion

    Kevin and his awesome hackathon team implemented Google maps functionality onto the website. We will integrate Twilio, Facebook, and other API's in order to have a robust website our members can use effectively.

  • TBA

    Website is finished and is ready for use. Kevin and his team will work to search and contact homeless people in the city of Milpitas, CA and Santa Cruz, CA. Outrach team helps spread the word and incorporate our technologies in homeless shelters and local libraries.

  • TBA++

    Future Phase Two Expansion

    Success in Milpitas and Santa Cruz have given Give Hope the confidence to expand their team in order to benefit neighboring cities & towns
